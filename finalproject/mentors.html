The ASH Foundation

What is ASH?

ASH is a non-profit organization that targets high school students who want an idea of what major they'd like to choose for college. We pair a college student who majors in a field that the high school student would like to learn about.

How it works?

We will manually pair a High school student and a college student based on a common major that they both put in our survey. The high school student and the college student will meet online at least 4 times a month and meet once a month in person.
