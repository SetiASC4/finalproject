Values
Our Values

Establish Your Future

Here at ASH, we want our high school students to have the mindset that they can make their future better no matter the income that their parents make.

Think broadly

Students at ASH have the opportunity to try out new majors that they find interesting. Because this is a free opportunity to try out new things, we would like students to have the mindset of being open to studying in majors that they may not have thought of before.

Taking risks

Usually, unforeseen opportunities come from risk-taking. Taking risks shows confidence and helps you stand out and even if you fail from taking a risk, you still learn from your failure.

Create Opportunities

Networking is a huge aspect in finding jobs in various fields. ASH helps both the mentor and mentee when it comes to networking by having everyone in ASH connect. Once you are accepted into ASH, you will always be part of ASH. Eventually, ASH will help find internships for our mentors and mentees.
